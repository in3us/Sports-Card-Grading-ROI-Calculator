# Sports and Pokemon Card Grading ROI Calculator
This spreadsheet is designed to evaluate the profitability of buying raw sports and Pokemon cards and submitting them for professional grading. It helps translate raw purchase prices and grading outcomes into expected ROI by modeling gem rates, grading costs, and sale prices.

[Sports Card Grading ROI Calculator](https://1drv.ms/x/c/5e0b3f01f8fd7272/IQB4BnuYE-QPSZDdeKoW4FAmAbtSEYE-BpE12GexmO1RUwQ)

# What it does

- Estimates profit and ROI for graded cards based on:

  - Raw purchase price

  - All-in grading costs
    
  - Probability of card receiving a gem mint (PSA 10) grade

  - Expected sale prices

- Compares successful (gem) vs non-gem outcomes to show true risk/reward

- Helps determine whether a raw card is worth grading before purchasing

# Sheet breakdown

- ROI
  - Summary view that highlights expected returns sorted by ROI from highest to lowest.
 
- Gem Rate
  - Manually created library of gem rates to assist in ROI calculation by understanding how likely a card is to grade a PSA 10 using data from gemrate.com.

- Cards
  - Input raw card data (30 day sale history from Market Movers for all raw, PSA 10, and PSA 9 collectibles with at least one sale).

- Calculator
  - Calculates profit, fees, and ROI for different grading outcomes.
